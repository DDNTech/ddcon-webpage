Costs

This year, registration will occur in three blocks: early, regular, and late. The prices for each are outlined below. Unlike last year, we will not have flash sales available – for the best pricing, we suggest signing up as soon as possible!

Early* (Mar 8 – Apr 9) Regular* (Apr 10 – May 31) Late* (June 1 - 30)
3 Day Pass $100 $100 $100
1 Day Pass $20 $30 $30

Note that registration is considered incomplete until your payment has been made. If you register within a certain block, but do not pay before the block deadline, your registration will be moved to the next block, which will require you to pay a higher price. Secure your spot when you register by completing your payment simultaneously!
