Full Weekend Pass

The Full Weekend Pass gives you the entire DDCON experience! It includes:

  • A two-night stay at Embassy Suites (Friday check-in/Sunday check-out; with 3 roommates)
  • An Exclusive Masters Class and Mixer on Friday
  • Access to at least 3 workshops, 2 panels, and 4 Master classes
  • 4 meals (Friday dinner, Saturday breakfast, Saturday lunch/dinner, Sunday breakfast)
  • Transportation to and from facilities for Saturday events
  • Exclusive DDCon Swag

Saturday Pass

The Saturday Pass gives you a whole day of DDCON fun! It includes:

  • Access to at least 3 workshops, 1 panel, and 2 Master classes
  • 1 meal (Saturday lunch/dinner)
  • Transportation to and from facilities for Saturday events
  • Exclusive DDCon Swag
