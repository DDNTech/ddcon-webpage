About DDCON

Desi Dance Convention, also known as DDCON, is an annual event dedicated to promoting South Asian performance art. Through a combination of dance workshops, informative panels, professional development seminars, and mixers, DDCON aims to provide an unforgettable experience for its guests. Instructors and speakers from both the Desi dance circuit and the professional field are personally selected to ensure a high-quality event and to inspire our guests to chase their artistic dreams. We are committed to creating an energetic and inclusive environment, in which South Asian artists can freely explore and develop their talents. Through our efforts to preserve this beautiful culture, we hope DDCON will play a pivotal role in encouraging collaboration and innovation within our creative community. Finally, we invite you to Dance, Discover, and Connect with us at DDCON 2017 – play on!
